what is SafeHer?

SafeHer is a tracker app created to support and connect women, allowing them to map out and share unpleasant or violent encounters during their daily routes.Violence against women is a recurrent issue, and we often feel unsafe just by walking down the street. With this app, your input can be a powerful tool to help women, by letting them know your experiences, where they happened, and by marking the places that made you feel unsafe.With SafeHer, you can also use the map to get to your destinations and track how you felt during your routes.

Together we can be safer!
