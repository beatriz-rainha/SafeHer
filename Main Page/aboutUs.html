who are we?

This project is the combined effort of three Communication Design finalist students: Beatriz, Maria and Thiago. We wanted to create a tool which could be useful and helpful on a daily basis, while also being of significant social relevance. Approaching some of our own issues and insecurities about the world and how it works, we came up with SafeHer.
